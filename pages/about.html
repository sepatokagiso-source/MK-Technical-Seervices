Our Vision

About MK Technical Services

MK Technical Services is a 100% Black-owned business founded in the Republic of South Africa specializing in Air-conditioning, Refrigeration, Electrical, BMS & Controls.

Our professional services range from Installation, servicing and repairs of Air-conditioners, Chillers, AHU, Fan-coil, Evaporation Condensers, cooling towers, down blowers, Split units /VRV and packaged units.

We are a legal entity registered with CIPC and also a certified Level 1 BBBEE contributor. This entity is run and managed by black professionals with financial accounting services qualifications and experience. Our company was founded in 2014 by sub-contracting privately and was officially registered in 2018.
