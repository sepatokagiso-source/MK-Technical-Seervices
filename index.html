About MK Technical Services

A 100% Black-owned business founded in the Republic of South Africa, specializing in Air-conditioning, Refrigeration, Electrical, BMS & Controls.

Our professional services range from Installation, servicing and repairs of Air-conditioners, Chillers, AHU, Fan-coil, Evaporation Condensers, cooling towers, down blowers, Split units /VRV and packaged units.

We are a legal entity registered with CIPC and a certified Level 1 BBBEE contributor. Founded in 2014, officially registered in 2018.
